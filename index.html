Got Questions?

What is Universite?

Universite is a platform that helps students find courses.We also act as a career guidance counsellor using artificial intelligence. 

Is Universite free to use?

Yes, Universite is completely free for students.

How does the AI assistant work?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to answer questions about courses, careers, and more.The chatbot is still under development and will be launched soon!

How do I apply and enroll for courses?

Universite displays courses from online course providers and public institutions.When you Click the apply button on a course you will be redirected to the course provider's online platform where you will enter the required details to apply/enroll for their course.
